<p>You may often hear the phrase: "AI is a black box." We know the input and output, but it's difficult to understand the operational mechanism in between. This opacity not only limits AI's application scope but also raises concerns about AI safety and reliability.</p><p>Anthropic's recent research aims to break open this black box, exploring the internal thought processes of AI models, paving a new path toward improving AI explainability and controllability.</p>
